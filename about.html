Our Story

Founded in January 2024 by Mohammed Darras, Crossing Bridges began with a vision to empower immigrants by providing support and resources essential for their successful integration into new communities. Mohammed, who experienced firsthand the challenges associated with moving to a new country, realized the significant impact that well-guided support can have on the lives of immigrants.

Mohammed's passion for helping others led him to create Crossing Bridges, an initiative designed to address the unique needs of immigrants through tailored programs and compassionate support. Our mission is to ease the transition for immigrants by helping them overcome the numerous challenges they face, from cultural adaptation and language barriers to employment and legal issues.

Crossing Bridges offers a range of services including stress management workshops, legal aid, language tutoring, and cultural assimilation activities. By focusing on these critical areas, we aim to reduce the burden of adjustment and promote a sense of belonging and community among immigrants.

Join us as we work together to make a difference, one life at a time. Whether you're an immigrant seeking support, a volunteer eager to help, or a donor looking to contribute, there's a place for you at Crossing Bridges. Together, we can bridge the gaps and build a stronger, more inclusive society.
